About Us

Life in Sundarbans website deals with the hardships faced by people who live in Sundarbans.The misery that they face in their everyday life due to the regular natural calamities,doesn't let them live a tension free life.This website has tried to lend a helping hand towards those unfortunate people by telling their story to the people accross the world. Any person, who lives far away from them,can easily help those people by donating through this website,and even a small donation means a lot.
